PANCAKE

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter. It is a type of batter bread.

Ingredients

Ingredients Measurements
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1/4 cup
Liquid milk 1/2 cup
Egg 1 Large
Baking powder 1 tsp
Salt a pinch
Butter 1 tsp
Groundnut oil 2 tsp

HOW TO MAKE PANCAKE

  • In a bowl, add all your dry ingredients including butter.
  • Follow with your wet ingredients (egg, milk).
  • Stir properly until you achieve the right consistency for your pancake choice. (Add ater if necessary)
  • Heat your pan and add in a tablespoon of oil.
  • Pour in your paste and allow to spread
  • Turn over once bules start to form at the top of your pancakes and then leave for 2-3 minutes.
  • Take out of pan and enjoy!
